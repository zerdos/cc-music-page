About

Collect Call is the dream-pop project of multi-instrumentalist Joseph Thorpe, based in Brighton, UK. Blending shoegaze, indie rock, and romantic otherworldly sounds, the music creates dreamlike atmospheres that explore themes of connection, reflection, and the transient beauty of everyday moments.

"Collect Call is a balm for our troubled times"
— Undercurrent Magazine

Drawing inspiration from vintage aesthetics, natural light, and the subtle narratives of urban landscapes, each song seeks to evoke a sense of nostalgic contemplation. From the quiet observation of "Rain:Stop" to the introspective gaze of "The Golden Hour," experience the world through a lens that finds profound meaning in the seemingly ordinary.

Music

Forever Reality

A hypnotic blend of nostalgic dream pop and shoegaze, exploring themes of connection, memory, and the surreal nature of modern communication.

Pretense

An exploration of perspective and the art of looking beyond the surface, featuring introspective lyrics and lush instrumentation.

In a Lilac Evening

Soft melodies capturing the serene beauty of a twilight sky, with gentle shoegaze textures and dreamy atmospherics.

The Golden Hour

An evocative EP journey through the fleeting magic of twilight. Features: Chase the Light, Simple, No Amount of Medicine, The Golden Hour.

Rain:Stop

The debut single that drew widespread attention and high praise. A poignant reflection on urban solitude and the pause found in unexpected moments.
